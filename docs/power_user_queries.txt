POWER USER QUERIES FOR READI AGENT (CATEGORIZED BY ROLE)

CATEGORIZED QUERIES:

1. PIC (Pilot in Command) - Compliance & Flight Limits
------------------------------------------------------
1. Analyze my last flight (Mission #1234)—did my max altitude of 140m violate the EU standard category limits?
2. Based on the DJI manual in our database, tell me if I should fly today with a battery temperature of 45°C?
3. How do I restart the mission if I lose the GCS link? Are there any specific safety report types I need to file for this?
4. Check my current certifications—am I legally allowed to fly the heavy-lift drone scheduled for tomorrow's mission?
5. Review the wind conditions for today's flight—do they exceed the 25km/h threshold mentioned in our tool's safety specs?

2. SM (Safety Manager) - Auditing & Risk Mitigation
---------------------------------------------------
6. Cross-reference all "Critical" safety reports this month with mission logs. Are there any common weather alerts during these incidents?
7. Which mission IDs were marked "Completed" today without a corresponding "Pre-Flight Check OK" boolean in the database?
8. Analyze the trend of "Link Loss" alerts over the last 90 days. Does this indicate a systemic issue with our current planning logic?
9. Show me missions from the last 7 days that took place in "Restricted Zones"—were the proper declarations filed for these?
10. Find the incident response procedure for "GPS interference" in our operator manual and tell me if its instructions were followed in last week’s report.

3. OPM (Operations Manager) - Efficiency & SLA Compliance
---------------------------------------------------------
11. Are we meeting our Client SLA of "95% mission success" this quarter? Which specific client has the highest failure rate?
12. List the top 3 drones with the highest flight hours—do any of them have active "Maintenance Tickets" that haven't been resolved?
13. Identify pilots who have flown over 20 hours this week but lack the "Advanced Night Flight" certification required for next week’s ops.
14. Compare the "Scheduled Start" vs. "Actual Start" for all missions this month. What is our average departure delay?
15. Do we have enough flight-ready tools available for the 5 missions scheduled for tomorrow across all organizations?

4. MM (Maintenance Manager) - Asset Management
----------------------------------------------
16. Show me drones that have crossed the 50-hour flight threshold since their last calibration but don't have a ticket yet.
17. Calculate the "Cost per Flight Hour" for the fleet based on the maintenance tickets filed in the last 6 months.
18. Flag any drones currently marked as "Tool Active: Y" that have an unresolved "High Priority" maintenance ticket.
19. Based on the manufacturer's expected lifespan, which motors or components need replacement within the next 30 days?
20. Which tools have their "Warranty Expiry" in the next 3 weeks? Do any of them currently have open defects?

SUMMARY OF HYBRID LOGIC:
Each of these queries requires the agent to combine database records (missions, tools, alerts) with external regulations (manuals, license rules). This proves the agent's ability to act as a compliance auditor rather than just a data search bar.
