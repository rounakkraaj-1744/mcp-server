**Remote Unmanned Operations U-Space Services Advisoring and Training**

**1. Mission Planning and Communication**

* **GO.00.02**: Mission planning and communication of activity completion
* The Pilot in Command (PIC) and the Requester (RDO) shall ensure that mission planning and communication of activity completion are properly executed.
* **GO.00.P01** and **GO.00.P02**: Communication of positive outcome and communication of update
* **GG.00.C01.01** and **GG.00.C01.04**: Communication of mission planning completion and communication of test completion
* **GO.00.P05**, **GO.00.P03**, and **GO.00.P04**: Communication of negative outcome, request for repetition of test, and mission assignment to the PIC

**2. Operation Management**

* **GO.01.01**: Insertion of missions
* **GO.01.02**: Pre-flight inspection and briefing
* The PIC and the RDO shall ensure that pre-flight inspection and briefing are properly executed.
* **GO.01.P01** and **GO.01.P02**: Communication of mission insertion and start of pre-flight phase
* **PRONTI AL DECOLLO**: Communication of readiness for takeoff

**3. Operation Management**

* **GO.02.04**: Control of flight
* **GO.02.02**: Start of mission
* **GO.02.05** and **GO.02.06**: Management of contingencies and emergencies
* The PIC and the RDO shall ensure that flight control, start of mission, and management of contingencies and emergencies are properly executed.
* **GO.02.03** and **GO.02.07**: Communication of mission KO and communication of mission completion
* **GG.02.C01.03** and **GG.02.C01.04**: Communication of mission KO and communication of mission completion

**4. Operation Management**

* **GO.03.01**: Start of post-flight procedure
* **GO.03.02**: Compilation of Checklist and QTB
* **GO.03.03**: Communication of mission completion
* The PIC, the Maintenance Manager (RMAN), and the RDO shall ensure that post-flight procedure, compilation of Checklist and QTB, and communication of mission completion are properly executed.
* **GO.03.05** and **GO.03.06**: Activation of maintenance service and verification of sensitive data
* **GG.03.C01.01**, **GG.03.C01.02**, and **GG.03.C01.03**: Communication of mission outcome, activation of verification of sensitive data, and activation of maintenance service
* **GO.03.04** and **GO.03.07**: Safety/AM activation and internal investigation activation

**5. Maintenance Management**

* **GM.01.02**: Receipt of maintenance ticket
* **GM.01.03**: Printing and shipping of label
* The Maintenance Manager and the Vendor shall ensure that receipt of maintenance ticket, printing and shipping of label, and opening of maintenance ticket are properly executed.
* **GM.01.01** and **GM.01.04**: Opening of maintenance ticket and preparation for pickup
* **GM.01.05** and **GM.01.06**: Assistance from vendor and readiness for shipping of repaired tool
* **GM.01.07** and **GM.01.08**: Receipt and testing of tool and receipt of maintenance request
* **GM.01.10**: Communication of completion of maintenance intervention

**6. Maintenance Management**

* **GM.02.00**: Receipt of maintenance request
* **GM.02.02** and **GM.02.03**: Maintenance intervention and update of tool status
* The Maintenance Manager shall ensure that receipt of maintenance request, maintenance intervention, and update of tool status are properly executed.
* **GM.02.04** and **GM.02.05**: Update of maintenance test register and update of tool status on management platform

**7. Training Management**

* **PR-TRN-01**: Process of managing UAS pilot training
* The Training Manager shall ensure that identification of training needs, planning of training, delivery of training, and registration of training evidence are properly executed.
* **PR-TRN-01**: Update of pilot training register and monitoring of pilot competencies

**8. Technical Logbook Management**

* **PR-TLB**: Process of managing Technical Logbook
* The Pilot in Command (PIC) shall ensure that nomination of Technical Logbook responsible, compilation of Technical Logbook, and check of anomalies are properly executed.
* **PR-TLB**: Update of Technical Logbook and start of maintenance process

**9. Maintenance Management**

* **PR-MNT-DCK-01**: Process of managing maintenance and maintenance program for DJI Dock 2 and associated UAS
* The Maintenance Technician shall ensure that maintenance intervention, compilation of maintenance sheet, and update of maintenance registers are properly executed.
* **PR-MNT-DCK-01**: Authorization for return to service and operational evidence
* **PR-MNT-02**: Update of Technical Logbook and maintenance register
